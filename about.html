My story

I originally started my career path with an Associates’ degree in Graphic Design. I have 6 years of experience in Graphic Design specifically in retail work. While working as a Graphic Designer I’ve developed skills in branding, animation, photography, and illustration that have transitioned well into UI design.

As a User Interface designer, I want to find projects that will challenge my creative thinking and push me to be overall a better visual designer. To go beyond my usual day of just designing to digging into the research and user testing so that I can deliver more than just a “pretty design” but a design that will function properly and best serve the user.
